## Long Description

### Turn your store into a custom order powerhouse

Custom File Upload Pro lets your customers attach files directly to their orders — perfect for personalised products, custom printing, bespoke design work, and any business that needs files from buyers.

**Set up in minutes, not hours.** Install the app, create an upload field, and it appears automatically on your product pages — no code required.

---

### Key Features

**🖼 Smart File Validation**
Accept exactly what you need. Set rules for file type (images, PDFs, videos, ZIP files), file size, image dimensions, aspect ratio, and DPI. Customers see clear error messages when files don't meet your requirements.

**🔒 Enterprise-Grade Security**
Every uploaded file is automatically scanned for viruses with ClamAV before your team can access it. Files are stored encrypted in AWS S3 with time-limited signed URLs — your customers' data is always safe.

**📐 Built-in Image Editor**
Let customers crop and rotate images right in your storefront before uploading, so you always receive properly formatted files.

**🎯 Flexible Assignment**
Show upload fields on specific products, product variants, collections, tags, or your entire store. Mix and match to build exactly the right experience for each product type.

**⚙️ Conditional Logic**
Show or hide upload fields based on which product tag, variant, or collection is selected — powerful enough for complex product catalogues.

**📦 Order Management**
Every upload is linked to its order automatically. Access all customer files from a clean dashboard, preview images, and download individual files or an entire order's attachments in one click.

**🌍 Multilingual Support**
The upload widget speaks your customers' language. Built-in translations for English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and Japanese, with custom message overrides for every piece of text.

**📊 Analytics Dashboard**
Track uploads over time, monitor storage usage, and spot trends with daily and monthly charts.

## Privacy Policy Key Points

- Files uploaded by customers are stored encrypted on AWS S3 in the US (or EU upon request)
- Files are scanned for viruses automatically
- Files are accessible only to the merchant via time-limited signed URLs
- Merchants may delete files at any time
- Files are deleted within 30 days of app uninstall
- We do not sell or share customer data with third parties
- GDPR compliant — data deletion requests honoured within 30 days
