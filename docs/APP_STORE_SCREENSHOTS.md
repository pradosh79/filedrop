## App Store Listing Full Description

**Custom File Upload Pro** lets Shopify merchants collect files from customers during the purchasing process — perfect for personalized products, print-on-demand, custom engraving, and more.

**Key Features:**

- 📁 **Flexible Field Types** — Image, PDF, Video, ZIP, Document, or any custom file type
- 🎨 **Built-in Image Editor** — Customers can crop, rotate, and preview images before uploading
- 📱 **Mobile Camera Upload** — Native camera capture on any phone
- 🔍 **Smart Assignment** — Show upload fields for specific products, variants, collections, tags, or your entire store
- ✅ **Image Validation** — Enforce minimum/maximum dimensions, aspect ratios, and resolution
- 🛡️ **Virus Scanning** — Every file is automatically scanned with ClamAV
- 📦 **Order Integration** — Uploaded files appear directly inside the Shopify order admin
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard** — Track uploads, storage usage, and trends over time
- 🌐 **6 Languages** — English, Spanish, German, French, Italian, Japanese
- 🔒 **Secure Storage** — Files stored in AWS S3 with expiring signed URLs

**How it works:**

1. Create an upload field and assign it to your products
2. The upload widget automatically appears on your product pages
3. Customers upload files before adding to cart
4. Files are attached to the order — view and download them right in Shopify
